The Problem

Most organizations are still running critical document workflows through tools they do not control.

Contracts are signed in one SaaS app.

Documents are drafted in another.

Records are stored across inboxes, drives, and portals.

Audit trails are hard to centralize.

Vendor pricing keeps increasing.

And if policy, procurement, or compliance requirements change, the organization is stuck adapting around someone else’s platform.

For EU-focused organizations, this creates a bigger issue:

You do not fully control the code.
You do not fully control the data.
You do not fully control the infrastructure.
You do not fully control the roadmap.

That is the gap this offer solves.
